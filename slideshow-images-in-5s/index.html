Is a Cat Right for You?

While cats make purr-fect pets for some people, they don’t for others. Cats are dependent on their owners for all their needs — food, water, medical attention, exercise, shelter, and, most important, companionship. For many people, the years of unconditional love and devotion they receive from their furry “kids” far outweigh the daily responsibilities. But for others, their lifestyles make it impossible.
